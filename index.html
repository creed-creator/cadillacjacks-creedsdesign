Our Story

Shelbyville's Favorite
Night Spot

Voted Shelbyville's best night spot, Cadillac Jack's is the downtown anchor for good food, cold drinks, and great times. Located right on the Public Square and open seven days a week.

From scratch-made wings and hand-crafted burgers to our full pizza menu and live weekly entertainment — there's always a reason to stop in.
